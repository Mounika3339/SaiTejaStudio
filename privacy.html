Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your information.

Home / Privacy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

The Best Pic (SaiTeja Digitals) ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details — name, phone number, email address, and event details you provide through our forms.
  • Communication records — messages exchanged via WhatsApp, phone, or email.
  • Usage data — anonymized browsing data such as pages visited and time spent on our site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide photography services.
  • Send package details, quotes, and availability information.
  • Deliver edited photos, albums, and related deliverables.
  • Improve our website and services.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and accessed only by authorized team members. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Photos and client data are retained as needed to provide our services and are stored on secure, password-protected systems.

5. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. We may use essential local storage for features like theme preference (dark mode). No third-party advertising cookies are used.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites (Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

8. Changes to This Policy
